Motivational Talks

I conducted a series of motivational talks for students with disabilities and their parents at three institutions – the Helen Keller Institute, Ummeed Child Development Centre and the Victoria Memorial School for the Blind, in Mumbai, India. Topics included: how to be a self-advocate and take help and abilities beyond disabilities for students, dreaming big for your kids (parents) and how to integrate in mainstream schooling for parents and students

Curriculum design

In order to increase computer literacy in students with disabilities, I am creating a curriculum in collaboration with the Helen Keller Institute, consisting of hot keys and shortcuts in various daily use computer applications such as word and excel, as well as commands to manipulate screen readers and make reading more accessible.
